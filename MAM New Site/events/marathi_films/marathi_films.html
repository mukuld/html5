Marathi Chitrapat Mahotsav

We are extremely pleased to bring two super hit Marathi movies to Twin Cities
  1. Mee Shivaji Raje Bhosale Boltoy
  2. Made in China
These movies have been very popular in India and elsewhere, where they were screened. It would not be an exaggeration to say that this event is expected to be a sellout, as it is open for members and non-members. For members, we strongly recommend that you RSVP at your earliest and before June 4, 2009, so that we can hold your tickets. The details of the event are:

Name of the Movie Show Time Star Cast
Mee Shivaji Raje Bhosale Boltoy 1:30 PM to 4:10 PM Sachin Khedekar, Mahesh Manjrekar, Makarand Anaspure, Siddharth Jadhav, Priya Bapat
Made in China 4:40 PM to 7:20 PM Sandeep Kulkarni, Milind Gunaji, Mrinal Kulkarni, Upendra Limaye, Madhura Velankar, Dr Sharad Bhutadiya and Shilpa Navalkar

We are all excited for this event and looking forward to enjoy to the fullest. The price structure for the movies is:

  • Members
    • $5 per person (ages 5 and above) for the event, whether you choose to watch one or both movies.
      Note: This is non-transferable
    • Member Guest (as defined in here): $5 for the event, whether you choose to watch one or both movies.
    • Kids under age of 5: FREE
  • Non-Members
    • $12 per movie or $20 for both movies
    • Non-Member Kids of ages between 5 and 12: $5 for the event, whether you choose to watch one or both movies
    • Non-member Kids under age of 5: FREE
  • MAM is not responsible for seating arrangements. Seats will be available on first come, first served basis
  • Theater policies do not allow MAM to provide food. However, theater concession stand will have light Indian snacks, tea, coffee, etc. for purchase.
NOTE: NO BABY SITTING ARRANGEMENTS AVAILABLE
