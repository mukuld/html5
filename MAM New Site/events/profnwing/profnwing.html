Professional Networking Event Registration

We are pleased to announce our first professional networking event.

Traditionally, we have been organizing social and cultural events, bonding our community in its own unique way. As we have grown in strength, so have we grown in our professional standing and our members have grown in the corporate and business world.

We think the opportunity is ripe for taking the next step for the community in providing a platform for professional networking to the Marathi community in and around twin cities.
All of us realize the value and importance of networking - and its more true in the current economic environment than ever before.
