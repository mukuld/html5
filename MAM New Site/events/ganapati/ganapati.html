Mangalmurti Morya

Traditionally "Ganesh Festival" is hosted by Marathi Association of Minnesota for all the communities. MAM is happy to host this year's Ganesh festival at the Hindu Mandir in Maple Grove. We are anticipating a large turnout for this festival, especially for “Sthapana” and “Visarjan”.Your active participation in making this community event successful is greatly appreciated.

Ganesh Sthapana Sunday August 23, 2009
Ganesh Visarjan Saturday, August 29, 2009
Venue: Hindu Temple of Minnesota

Program Outline:
Ganesh Sthapana, Sunday August 23, 2009

Ganapati Procession - Please arrive at 9:30am in the temple. Lezim is planned. Please arrive early to participate in this Marathi tradition. 10:00 AM
Ganesh Sthapana - Sri Jignesh Joshi 10:30 AM
Arati and Prasad 12:00 Noon

Ganesh festival decorations: We will be putting up decorations in the temple on Saturday evening August 22nd at 4pm. Volunteers welcome. Contact Seema Godbole for details.

Prasad Lunch: We are expecting over 1500 devotees during Sthapana day. We will need volunteers for cooking and serving. Seema Godbole will be coordinating the cooking team. Please contact her if you are interested in helping with lunch preparation and serving.

Daily Pooja and Arati: Evening at 7.00 PM between Sthapana to Visarjan (Note: Temple closes at 8.30 PM).

If you are interested in performing Ganesh Pooja during weekdays of Ganapati festival, please contact Seema Godbole

Ganesh Visarjan, Saturday August 29, 2009

Atharvashirsha recital: Priya Shilotri 9:00 AM - 10:30 AM
Pooja - Sri Jignesh Joshi 10:30 AM
Arati and Lunch* 12:00 Noon
Visarjan 2:30 PM

* - Hindu Mandir charges a nominal amount for Lunch.

Note:

  • To facilitate smooth operation, only MAM prasad will be distributed upstairs. All other Prasad will be distributed in Lunch hall along with the food.
  • Devotees are encouraged to bring customary Modak or Saran (coconut/Good mixture) as Prasad. Other types of dry Prasads/whole fruits are fine.
  • Please bring all the prepared prasad to the temple in aluminum trays or other disposable containers.

We will be accepting cash donations to buy raw materials for preparations of Prasad lunch during both Sthapana and Visarjan days. Please make checks payable to MAM and send them to Samir Junnarkar at 6698 Urbandale LN N, Maple Grove MN 55311
