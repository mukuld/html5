Events Calendar

The Marathi Association organizes a spectrum of programs throughout the year. The six main programs organized are, Makar Sankrant, Vasant Bahar, Camping/Cruise, Picnic, Ganesh Sthapana-Satsung and Diwali. For each program, the committee assigns two families to coordinate the program along with the committee. In addition, Committee organizes programs performed by visiting guests from India.
