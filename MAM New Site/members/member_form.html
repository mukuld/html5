  1. Membership Fees are non-refundable and non-transferrable.
  2. The Membership type and fee listed above is for year 2008 - 09, and is valid from Diwali function of 2008 and expires on Diwali function of 2009.
  3. Membership includes all main programs at no additional costs. These programs typically are Sankrant, Vasant Bahar, Picnic/Camping, Ganapati Festival, and Diwali. Depending upon the funds availability and time, more event(s) will be offered free for all members. All members are eligible for discounted rates for Outside Artist programs
  4. You need to be a member or pay non-member's charges to attend/perform in the cultural programs/events organized by MAM.
  5. Members can bring any guests to any event.
    • Guests residing in Minnesota will have to pay non-member charges which will be based upon the event.
    • Guest residing outside of Minnesota will have to pay a discounted charge which will be based on the event. These guests will have to produce a valid ID upon request.
  6. Students - Even if there is no Students' category, they are welcome to all MAM events, internal or external. However, depending on the program, they will be charged a low-discounted rate as long as they are a full-time student and, if applicable, has spouse who is non-earning. Students will have to produce a VALID Student ID to benefit from this privilege
