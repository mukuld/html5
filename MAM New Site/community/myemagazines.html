My e-Magazines - Your source for Marathi Literature online

The good folks at My e-Magazines are working diligently to spread the magic of Marathi magazines among a greater readership, which will ensure their presence in the market for future generations. As of now, many good Marathi magazines have discontinued publication due to a lack of readership and finance, because today’s generation demands more English periodicals. Consequently, many good writers have ceased to submit their work due to a lack of career assurance and therefore have become unmotivated.

Please visit the website, www.myemagazines.com, and click on "Prashansa" to know that Marathi newspapers and other media are voicing this same opinion.

Please help in spreading the word among your mandal members or you may forward the link to this page to them for their information.

Please visit the My e-Magazines website to learn more about the activities.

Currently, there are 27 monthly magazines and 50 Diwali Ank to read online. Few more are in pipeline.

Please share this information with your friends and relatives, who love reading Marathi. We are sure they will be thankful to you for informing them about this.

We highly appreciate your help in this regard!
