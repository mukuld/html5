Marathi Association of Minnesota

Proudly Presents

Vasant Bahaar

Venue: Cafetorium @ Valley View Middle School (Directions)

Address: 8900 Portland Ave S
                Bloomington, MN 55420

Date: 04/11/2009

Time: 5:00 pm to 9:00 pm

Program Details:

Ranga Rang: Amateur Talent Show. Entries invited for all kinds of entertainment event (Stand-up comedy, Instrumental, Vocal performance etc.)

Ukhanyachi Bahaar: Scratch your heads to come up with your own creative ukhaney or the ones you remember from olden times and get ready for a "Ukhaney Fight"! If your ukhana matches the theme of the evening (Spring, Holi, Shiv Jayanti, Gudhi padwa, etc) even better.

There are many prizes to be won. So get ready to have a great time...

Our annual attraction during Vasant Bahaar; the food stalls is there. Indulge your taste senses & polish your game playing skills for there will be a lot of stalls with some delectable foods and games to play...

Entrance Fee:

Members: Free

Non-Members: $5 (kids under 12 are free)
