MAM is proud to present Diwali 2009

The theme this year is to highlight the diverse cultural/regional art forms from India. The program content will be a balance of song and dance and other art forms both modern and from the glorious past.

We promise to bring back all of your favorite events, plus a few surprises. As always, you can expect to see some performances by our talented members (boys and girls, young and old) as well as a dizzying array of food, crafts and more! The celebration will culminate with a play/drama .

We would like your help in presenting a quality program and showcasing the talent amongst our community. Please participate in any solo or group events and encourage your kids too. Some suggestions are song and dance performances, short skits, musicals like Powada/garba/Qawali etc.

We await your offers and hope to accommodate all. Please send in your entries by September 10th 2009 (first come first served!).

Contact details: mamdiwali2009@gmail.com

Send us MP3 , information about programme , any questions over email.
