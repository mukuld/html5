Royal Bengal Tiger

The stripes of the tiger are said to be unique for an animal, just like our own fingerprints

  • Scientific Name: Panthera tigris
  • Average Weight: Around 400 pounds
  • Average Lifespan: 20 - 26 years (In captivity)
  • Habitat: The Sundarbans, India
  • Diet: Carnivore

The tiger is the largest cat species, most recognizable for their pattern of dark vertical stripes on reddish-orange fur with a lighter underside.
