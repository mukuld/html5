Jokes created by Jayant

My 9 year old son Jayant, likes to tell jokes a lot. More than telling jokes, he likes to create jokes. And believe me, his jokes are really funny for a 9 year old. I have been promising him that I will document them and publish them on my website and a book. So here I am doing the first part.

    Once the Sun invited the Moon over for dinner. The Sun ate heartily and gorged himself, but the Moon only took one serving. Why?

    Answer: She was a full moon.
